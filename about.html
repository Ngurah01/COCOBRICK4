About Our Company

Cocobrick is a producer and exporter of coconut shell charcoal briquettes from Indonesia that focuses on using environmentally friendly raw materials such as coconut shells. Our factory is located in Singaraja Bali, Indonesia, where we produce high quality charcoal briquettes and a sustainable production process. We are committed to providing high quality products that meet international standards while paying attention to environmental sustainability and the welfare of local communities. With our dedication to innovation and quality, Cocobrick is ready to be a trusted partner for you in meeting your needs for high quality and environmentally friendly coconut shell charcoal fuel.

We are proud to use 100% real coconut shell raw materials in every charcoal briquette we produce. Coconut shells are an abundant natural resource in Indonesia and are an ideal choice for making charcoal briquettes because of their environmentally friendly nature and high quality burnt products. We prioritize careful selection and processing of coconut shells to ensure consistent and superior quality of charcoal briquettes. By using real coconut shells, we can produce charcoal briquettes that have high burning power, low smoke, and are free from dangerous chemicals. With Cocobrick, you can trust your supply of high quality coconut shell charcoal briquettes that are environmentally friendly and safe to use.
